Our Story

RS Metal was established with a vision to provide reliable and high-quality metal products to the growing Indian market. Founded by Mr. Deepak R. Mehta, the company began operations in Mumbai, Maharashtra, and has since grown to become a trusted name in the metal trading industry.

With our GST registration in July 2017, we formalized our commitment to transparent business practices and compliance with Indian regulations. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with suppliers, manufacturers, and clients across various industries.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To be the most reliable supplier of metal products by ensuring consistent quality, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service. We aim to build long-term partnerships with our clients based on trust and mutual growth.

Vision: To become the preferred metal trading partner across India, known for our quality products, ethical business practices, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Values

  • Quality Excellence: We source only the highest quality materials from reputable manufacturers and suppliers.
  • Customer Focus: Our clients are at the center of everything we do. We work to understand their needs and provide tailored solutions.

  • Innovation: We continuously improve our processes and explore new products to better serve our clients.
